## 5 Markers of a "Citation-Worthy" Page

### 1. The Answer Capsule (The "Hook")

AI engines like Perplexity look for concise, definitive answers to user queries. Texavor identifies the "High-Intent Question" for your topic and drafts a direct answer block that LLMs can easily extract.

### 2. Entity Density & Relationships

Instead of "Keyword Stuffing," we use **Entity Density**. We help you link your brand (Entity A) to a specific problem (Entity B) and a verified solution (Entity C). This builds the "Semantic Map" that ChatGPT uses to understand your authority.

### 3. Structural Scannability for Bots

While humans read left-to-right, LLMs "read" via structural relationships. Our **Table Scanner** and **Entity Graphs** ensure that the data is presented in a format that AI agents can parse in milliseconds.

### 4. Technical Trust (Schema & JSON-LD)

Schema.org markup is the "handshake" between your site and an AI engine. We use custom JSON-LD generators for FAQs, brand authority, and content freshness to signal "this information is current and verified."

### 5. Social & Fact Integrity

AI engines cross-reference content with other sources. By integrating **Social Credibility** signals and **Fact-Check Scanners**, we ensure your content matches the "Consensus" while providing unique, citable data points.
