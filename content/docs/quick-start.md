# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with EasyWrite in just 5 minutes! This guide will walk you through the essential steps to start creating amazing content.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit the EasyWrite homepage
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"**
3. Complete your registration with email or social login
4. Verify your email address

## Step 4: Create Your First Article

### Using AI Topic Generation

1. Click **"AI Tools"** â†’ **"Topic Generator"**
2. Enter a broad keyword or theme
3. Review AI-generated topic suggestions
4. Click **"Create Article"** on your chosen topic

### Writing from Scratch

1. Click **"New Article"** from the dashboard
2. Enter your article title
3. Use the editor to write your content
4. Get AI suggestions as you type

### Using AI Writing Assistant

While writing:

- Highlight text and click **"Improve"** for AI suggestions
- Use **"Continue Writing"** to let AI complete your thoughts
- Click **"Generate Outline"** to structure your article

## Step 5: Optimize for SEO

1. Click **"SEO"** tab in your article editor
2. Enter a focus keyword
3. Review SEO recommendations:
   - Title optimization
   - Meta description
   - Keyword density
   - Readability score
4. Make suggested improvements

## Step 6: Generate a Thumbnail

1. While editing your article, navigate to the **"Thumbnail"** section
2. Click **"Generate with AI"**
3. Choose from generated options or customize:
   - Add text overlay
   - Adjust colors
   - Select template style
4. Click **"Apply Thumbnail"**

## Step 7: Publish Your Content

When you're ready to publish:

1. Review your article in **"Preview"** mode
2. Click **"Publish"** or **"Schedule"**
3. Choose publication options:
   - Publish immediately
   - Schedule for later
   - Save as draft
4. Confirm and publish!

## Common Questions

### How do I save my work?

Articles auto-save every few seconds. You'll see a "Saved" indicator in the editor.

### Can I edit published articles?

Yes! Navigate to your article and click "Edit" to make changes. Republish when ready.

### How many blogs can I connect?

You can connect multiple blogs depending on your plan. Check Settings â†’ Subscription for details.

### Where do I find my API credentials?

API credentials are specific to your blog platform. Check your blog's settings or documentation.
