**Target keywords that AI can actually answer.**

Traditional tools only show "Volume" and "Difficulty". Texavor adds a crucial third dimension: **AI Visibility**.

![Keyword Research Dashboard](/docs/keyword-research.png)

## 1. The Interface

Enter a seed topic (e.g., `generative engine optimization`) to see a list of related terms.

### Key Metrics

- **Volume:** Monthly search volume.
- **CPC:** Cost Per Click (Commercial Intent proxy).
- **Difficulty (0-100):** How hard it is to rank on Google.
- **AI Visibility:** _The Texavor Advantage._

### Search Modes

The toggle next to the search bar controls the depth of analysis:

- **Basic:** Clarified it returns related keywords without metrics (fast brainstorming).
- **Detailed:** Confirmed this is where Volume/Difficulty/CPC live.
- **Prompt:** Kept as the "AI Visibility" mode.
