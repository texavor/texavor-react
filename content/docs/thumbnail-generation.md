# Thumbnail Generation

Create eye-catching, professional thumbnails in seconds with AI-powered image generation. Design custom thumbnails that capture attention and reflect your brand identity.

## What is Thumbnail Generation?

EasyWrite's Thumbnail Generation uses AI to create:

- Professional blog post thumbnails
- Social media graphics
- Featured images
- Custom branded visuals
- Template-based designs

## Accessing Thumbnail Generator

**From Article Editor:**

1. Open any article
2. Navigate to **"Thumbnail"** tab
3. Click **"Generate with AI"**

**From Dashboard:**

1. Go to **AI Tools** ‚Üí **"Thumbnail Generator"**
2. Create standalone graphics

**Quick Action:**

1. Select article
2. Click **"Create Thumbnail"**

## Generating Thumbnails

### Method 1: AI-Powered Generation

Let AI create thumbnails based on your content:

**Steps:**

1. Click **"Generate with AI"**
2. AI analyzes your article:
   - Title and topic
   - Main keywords
   - Content theme
   - Target audience
3. View generated options (6-8 variants)
4. Select your favorite
5. Customize if needed
6. Apply to article

**Generation Speed:** ~10-15 seconds

### Method 2: Template-Based

Use pre-designed templates:

**Steps:**

1. Click **"Choose Template"**
2. Browse template library:
   - Minimal
   - Bold
   - Professional
   - Creative
   - Tech
   - Lifestyle
3. Select template
4. Customize elements:
   - Title text
   - Colors
   - Images
   - Fonts
5. Save thumbnail

### Method 3: Brand Style Analysis

Create thumbnails matching your brand:

**First Time Setup:**

1. Navigate to **Settings** ‚Üí **"Thumbnail Styles"**
2. Enter your website URL
3. Click **"Analyze Brand"**
4. AI extracts:
   - Color palette
   - Typography style
   - Design elements
   - Brand personality

**Using Brand Styles:**

1. Generate new thumbnail
2. Select **"Use Brand Style"**
3. AI creates on-brand designs automatically
4. All thumbnails match your aesthetic
