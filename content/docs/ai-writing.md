# AI Writing Assistant

Unlock the full potential of AI-powered writing with EasyWrite's intelligent writing assistant. Create high-quality content faster while maintaining your unique voice and style.

## What is the AI Writing Assistant?

The AI Writing Assistant is your intelligent writing companion that helps you:

- Generate content ideas and complete drafts
- Improve existing text for clarity and engagement
- Continue your thoughts when you hit writer's block
- Rewrite content in different tones and styles
- Expand or condense sections

## Getting Started with AI Writing

### Accessing AI Tools

The AI Writing Assistant is available throughout the content editor:

1. **Toolbar**: Access AI tools from the editor toolbar
2. **Context Menu**: Right-click selected text for AI options
3. **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Use `/` to open AI command palette
4. **AI Panel**: Open the side panel for advanced options

## Core AI Features

### 1. AI Content Generation

Generate complete article sections or full drafts:

**How to Use:**

1. Open a new article or select a section
2. Click **"Generate with AI"** or type `/generate`
3. Provide a prompt or topic description
4. Review and edit generated content

**Best Practices:**

- Be specific with your prompts
- Include target keywords
- Specify desired length (e.g., "500 words")
- Mention target audience

**Example Prompts:**

```
Write an introduction about the benefits of meditation for beginners

Create a step-by-step guide on setting up a WordPress blog

Explain cryptocurrency in simple terms for seniors
```

### 2. Continue Writing

Let AI complete your thoughts when you're stuck:

**How to Use:**

1. Write a few sentences
2. Click **"Continue Writing"** or press `Ctrl+Space`
3. AI analyzes context and continues your content
4. Accept or regenerate suggestions

**When to Use:**

- Experiencing writer's block
- Need inspiration for next points
- Want to explore different angles

### 3. Improve Text

Enhance existing content for better clarity and engagement:

**How to Use:**

1. Select text you want to improve
2. Click **"Improve"** or type `/improve`
3. Review AI suggestions
4. Accept changes or try again

**What Gets Improved:**

- Grammar and spelling
- Sentence structure
- Word choice and vocabulary
- Flow and readability
- Tone consistency

### 4. Rewrite Content

Transform your content into different styles or tones:

**Available Tones:**

- **Professional** - Formal business writing
- **Casual** - Friendly, conversational tone
- **Technical** - Detailed, precise language
- **Simple** - Easy-to-understand explanations
- **Persuasive** - Compelling, action-oriented
- **Storytelling** - Narrative, engaging style

**How to Use:**

1. Select text to rewrite
2. Click **"Rewrite"** → Choose tone
3. Review rewritten version
4. Apply or try different tone

### 5. Expand or Condense

Adjust content length while preserving meaning:

**Expand:**

- Add more details and examples
- Elaborate on concepts
- Increase word count

**Condense:**

- Create summaries
- Focus on key points
- Reduce word count

**How to Use:**

1. Select target text
2. Choose **"Expand"** or **"Condense"**
3. Specify desired length (optional)
4. Review and apply changes

## Advanced AI Features

### AI Outline Generator

Create structured article outlines:

1. Navigate to **"AI Tools"** → **"Outline Generator"**
2. Enter your topic or title
3. Specify:
   - Target word count
   - Number of sections
   - Target audience
4. Review generated outline
5. Click **"Create Article"** to start writing

**Example Output:**

```
Article: "Complete Guide to Plant-Based Nutrition"

I. Introduction
   - What is plant-based nutrition?
   - Why it's gaining popularity

II. Health Benefits
   - Heart health
   - Weight management
   - Disease prevention

III. Getting Started
   - Essential nutrients to track
   - Meal planning basics
   - Shopping guide

IV. Common Challenges
   - Protein concerns
   - Social situations
   - Budget considerations

V. Conclusion
   - Summary of key points
   - Action steps
```

### AI Title Suggestions

Generate compelling article titles:

1. Write your content or outline
2. Click **"Generate Titles"**
3. Review 10+ title suggestions
4. Select or customize your favorite

**Title Styles:**

- How-to guides
- Listicles
- Question-based
- Benefit-focused
- Curiosity-driven

### Smart Suggestions

Get real-time writing suggestions as you type:

**What You'll Get:**

- Keyword integration suggestions
- Sentence structure improvements
- Readability enhancements
- Tone adjustments
- Related topic suggestions

**Configure in Settings:**

- Toggle suggestion frequency
- Set preferred writing style
- Choose suggestion types

## Using AI Effectively

### Best Practices

**1. Provide Context**

- Give AI enough information
- Include relevant keywords
- Specify target audience
- Mention desired tone

**2. Edit AI Output**

- Always review generated content
- Add personal insights
- Verify facts and claims
- Maintain your unique voice

**3. Iterate and Refine**

- Generate multiple versions
- Combine best parts
- Refine with follow-up prompts

**4. Use as a Starting Point**

- Treat AI as inspiration
- Build upon suggestions
- Add expertise and examples

Speed up your AI writing workflow:

| Shortcut           | Action                  |
| ------------------ | ----------------------- |
| `Ctrl + Space`     | Continue writing        |

| `Ctrl + Shift + R` | Rewrite selected text   |
| `Ctrl + Shift + E` | Expand selected text    |

## Troubleshooting

### AI Not Generating Expected Output

**Try:**

- Make prompts more specific
- Provide more context
- Break complex requests into smaller parts
- Regenerate with different wording

### Generated Content Off-Topic

**Solutions:**

- Include keywords in prompt
- Specify exact requirements
- Use examples in your prompt
- Guide AI with structure

### Content Feels Generic

**Improvements:**

- Add personal anecdotes
- Include specific examples
- Insert statistics and data
- Incorporate expert quotes
