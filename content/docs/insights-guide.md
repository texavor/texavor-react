# Understanding Insights

Data-driven decisions lead to better content. EasyWrite's Insights feature gives you the metrics that matter.

## Key Metrics

- **Views**: How many people are seeing your content.
- **Engagement**: Likes, comments, and shares.
- **Read Time**: How long users stay on your pages.

## Improving Your Score

Our proprietary Content Score helps you gauge quality. To improve it:

1.  Use clear, concise language.
2.  Break up text with headings and images.
3.  Optimize for relevant keywords.
