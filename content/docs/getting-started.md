## Your First Article

### Create with AI

1. Click **"New Article"** from dashboard
2. Enter a topic or title
3. Click **"Generate with AI"**
4. Choose:
   - **Full Draft** - Complete article
   - **Outline Only** - Structure to fill in
   - **Blank** - Start from scratch
5. Edit and customize the content
6. Add a thumbnail
7. Optimize for SEO
8. Publish!

### Or Start from Scratch

1. Click **"New Article"**
2. Enter your title
3. Start writing in the editor
4. Use AI suggestions as you type
5. Format and style your content
6. Publish when ready
