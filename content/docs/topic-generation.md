# Topic Generation

Never run out of content ideas again! EasyWrite's AI-powered topic generation helps you discover trending topics, identify content gaps, and create a content calendar that resonates with your audience.

## What is Topic Generation?

Topic Generation uses AI and data analysis to suggest relevant, engaging content topics based on:

- Your niche and industry
- Trending searches and discussions
- Competitor content analysis
- Audience interests
- SEO opportunities

## Understanding Topic Metrics

Each generated topic includes:

### Relevance Score

How well the topic matches your niche:

- **90-100**: Perfect match
- **70-89**: Highly relevant
- **50-69**: Moderately relevant
- **Below 50**: Less relevant

### SEO Potential

Ranking opportunity analysis:

- **High**: Easy to rank, good volume
- **Medium**: Moderate effort needed
- **Low**: Highly competitive

### Content Gap Score

How well this topic fills content gaps:

- **High**: Few existing resources
- **Medium**: Some competition
- **Low**: Saturated topic

### Engagement Potential

Predicted audience interest:

- **High**: Trending, high social shares
- **Medium**: Steady interest
- **Low**: Niche interest
